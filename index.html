FARNELL NEWTON

Trumpeter Farnell Newton is one of the most versatile musicians in the Northwest, playing straight-ahead jazz, Latin music, soul, hip-hop, funk and more. Farnell is a graduate of Oberlin College/Conservatory of Music and Portland State University and was on the faculty of Portland State as a Professor of Jazz Trumpet. Currently, Farnell works in the A&R department at Marmoset which is a full-service music agency, curating a collection of rare, vintage and emerging artists, bands and record labels for music licensing. After years of performing with the Portland Cello Project, George Clinton and Parliament Funkadelic, Jill Scott, Bootsy Collins, Fred Wesley, Stevie Wonder, and many others. Farnell saw that it was time to get back into the tech industry and pursue a career as a web designer, consultant and advocate to bring more diversity into the tech industry by first enrolling in Epicodus Introduction to Programming.
